Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-01-12

textclip.sh is a static website that helps you generate copyable links/badges that embed text in the URL (via query params and/or the URL fragment).

What we collect

  • No account data: There are no user accounts.
  • No cookies: textclip.sh does not set cookies for tracking or advertising.
  • No analytics: We do not run client-side analytics scripts.
  • No server-side storage of your text: Your text stays in your browser and in the URL you generate. If you share the URL, anyone with the URL can see the embedded text.

Hosting / logs

textclip.sh is hosted on Cloudflare Pages. Like most hosting providers, Cloudflare may process request metadata (for example: IP address, user agent, and request timestamps) for security, reliability, and performance.

Third-party links

Generated links may redirect you to third-party services (for example, AI chatbots or search engines). Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies.

Contact

For questions, contact av.codes.
